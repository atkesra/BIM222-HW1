Isabelle Geffroy, better known by the nickname Zaz,is a French singer-songwriter

DINLE BENI BI

Artist:Yüzyüzeyken Konusuruz

Was established in May 2011. It was a project that consisted of the songs of a young

this duo was accompanied by various musicians.

YA SONRA

Artist:Levent Yüksel

Levent Yüksel is a Turkish pop singer and multi instrumentalist.

He has been active in live music industry since 1993.

Ya sonra the song is part of the album of Kadin Sarkilari published in 2006.

First time voiced by Ajda Pekkan. Lyrics of the song is belong to Fikret Senses.

ZOR KADIN

Artist:Sertap Erener

Sertab Erener is a Turkish singer, songwriter and composer.

With her coloratura soprano voice, she started working as a backing vocalist for Sezen Aksu,

and with Aksu's help she released her first studio album in the 1990s.

Lyrics of the song is belong to Aysel Gürel.

Learn More about Sertap Erener

SELF CONTROL

Artist:Sena Sener

Sena Sener, 19, has been showing her character as a musician

and a songwriter with her guitar in her hands, which she hasn't dropped

since the age of 9.Sena has released singles called "Bak Bana", "Ona",

"Sevmemeliyiz" which are alternative/rock songs reflecting her true

identity as a musician, under the name of Pasaj Müzik. Additionally,

she participated in the hit called "Feel" with Mahmut Orhan,

as the vocalist and the songwriter, and she did a collaboration

with Julius Abel with her song "Waiting On The Shore".
